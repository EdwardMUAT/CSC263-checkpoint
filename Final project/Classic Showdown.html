Description

The Classic Showdown has games like Tic-Tac-Toe and Rock-Paper-Scissors. You can play these games against the computer and have lots of fun!

Features

Tic-Tac-Toe

Instructions

Here's how to play the games:

  • Tic-Tac-Toe:
    1. Click "Play Tic-Tac-Toe" to start.
    2. The game board with a 3x3 grid will appear.
    3. Take turns clicking an empty cell to place your X or O.
    4. Get three X's or O's in a row to win!
    5. If all cells are filled and no one wins, it's a draw.
  • Rock-Paper-Scissors:
    1. Click "Play Rock-Paper-Scissors" to start.
    2. Type Rock, Paper, or Scissors when prompted.
    3. See if you beat the computer!
    4. Rock beats Scissors, Scissors beat Paper, and Paper beats Rock.
    5. If you both choose the same, it's a tie!
  • View Scores: Click to see your game scores.
  • Add Player: Click to add your name before playing.
  • Multi-Threading Example: See how things happen at the same time!
  • Chatbot Interaction: Say hi and see what happens!
